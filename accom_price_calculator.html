The locked display model
  • Per room-night (each room its own): originalPrice, subsidyDirect, subsidyCashback, tixPoint, tax.
  • Order-level, once: promoDirect, promoCashback, convenienceFee.
  • Subtotal = Σ originalPrice − discount − cashback (the "harga setelah cashback").
  • discount = ΣsubsidyDirect (+ promoDirect when promo). promoDirect can be a flat Rp amount or a % of the subtotal after direct subsidy (Σ originalPrice − Σ subsidyDirect), optionally capped at a max discount.
  • cashback = ΣsubsidyCashback + ΣtixPoint; promo forfeits tixPoint → ΣsubsidyCashback + promoCashback. Returned as Blibli Tiket Points; added back into the total (not deducted).
  • Total bayar = Subtotal + Tax + Cashback. Payment page adds the convenience fee → Total pay; Booking Form does not.
  • SRP shows Gimmick (struck) + Main (after cashback); the after-tax price appears as a caption under the before-tax filter, and can be viewed per room/night or as the full-stay total.
