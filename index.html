About this project

The Price Consistency Project: Hotel documents how a room price is displayed to the user across every screen of the hotel booking journey — from the search results page all the way to payment. Its goal is to make sure the price a customer sees stays consistent, transparent, and correctly labelled (per room/night, total, before or after tax, and with or without promo) at every step.

Each column above is one booking flow scenario. Use the arrows to step through its screens, drag the handle (☰) to reorder columns for side-by-side comparison, and open a section in the breakdown below to view all screens of a flow at once. The scenarios cover the key pricing variations:

  • Room/night vs. total pay — whether the headline price is per night or the full stay.
  • With tax vs. without tax — how taxes and fees are surfaced (🔴 without, 🟢 with).
  • With promo vs. without promo — how discounts and cashback affect the displayed price.
  • Single vs. multi-night / multi-room — how quantity is reflected in the total.

Screens are grouped by flow into folders and loaded automatically, so the gallery always reflects the latest design exports — no manual updates needed when a screen is added, renamed, or removed.
